Citrus Herb Crusted Salmon with Lemon-Herb Quinoa, Roasted Carrot-Ginger Tarts, and Citrus-Mint Aioli.

A vibrant and refreshing dish featuring a perfectly cooked salmon fillet crusted with a zesty citrus herb mixture, served atop a bed of fluffy lemon-herb quinoa. The plate is accompanied by two roasted carrot-ginger tarts, their golden-brown crusts giving way to a sweet and tangy filling. A dollop of creamy citrus-mint aioli adds a rich and indulgent touch to the dish.
