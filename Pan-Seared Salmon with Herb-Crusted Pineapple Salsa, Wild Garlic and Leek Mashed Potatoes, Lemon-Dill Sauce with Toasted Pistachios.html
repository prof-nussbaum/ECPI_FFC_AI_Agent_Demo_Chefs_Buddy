Pan-Seared Salmon with Herb-Crusted Pineapple Salsa, Wild Garlic and Leek Mashed Potatoes, Lemon-Dill Sauce with Toasted Pistachios.

Pan-seared salmon fillet, golden brown and cooked to perfection, sits atop a bed of creamy wild garlic and leek mashed potatoes. The dish is garnished with a vibrant herb-crusted pineapple salsa, bursting with fresh flavors and colors. A side of lemon-dill sauce adds a tangy and refreshing touch, complemented by toasted pistachios that add crunch and texture to the plate.
