Pan-Seared Salmon with Herbed Quinoa, Roasted Bell Peppers, and Balsamic Honey Glaze.

Indulge in the vibrant colors of this dish, where a perfectly seared salmon fillet is nestled atop a bed of herbed quinoa, its fluffy grains infused with fragrant herbs. Roasted bell peppers add a pop of color and sweetness, while a drizzle of rich balsamic honey glaze brings everything together in perfect harmony.
