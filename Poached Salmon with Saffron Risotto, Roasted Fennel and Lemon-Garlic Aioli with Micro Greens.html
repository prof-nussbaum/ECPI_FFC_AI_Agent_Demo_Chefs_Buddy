Poached Salmon with Saffron Risotto, Roasted Fennel and Lemon-Garlic Aioli with Micro Greens.

A delicate poached salmon fillet is artfully placed atop a bed of creamy saffron risotto, its golden hue glistening in the light. Roasted fennel adds a pop of anise flavor and crunchy texture, while a dollop of zesty lemon-garlic aioli brings a tangy contrast to each bite. Fresh micro greens add a burst of freshness, completing this visually stunning dish.
