Seared Salmon with Crispy Panko Cauliflower, Maple-Glazed Carrots, and Spicy Harissa Remoulade.

The seared salmon is placed atop a bed of vibrant greens, its golden-brown crust glistening in the light. To one side lies a delicate arrangement of maple-glazed carrots, their sweet and sticky glaze inviting all to take a bite. On the other side, a crispy Panko cauliflower florets dance with joy, their crunchy texture providing a delightful contrast to the tender salmon. And nestled between them, a dollop of spicy harissa remoulade adds a tangy kick to this already impressive dish.
